# Risk Policy - Northwind Brew

## Client Authority
Cody is the final decision maker. Agents can recommend, draft, and generate assets, but KISS must surface scope and verification risks before work expands.

## Low Risk - Agent May Proceed
- Copy edits for the one-page site.
- Visual polish on existing assets.
- Accessibility improvements.
- Updating KISS memory files after a demo action.

## Medium Risk - Check Scope First
- Adding a second page.
- Adding a new form field.
- Changing brand direction.
- Generating new assets that imply claims about real products or locations.

## High Risk - Warn Or Park
- Ecommerce checkout.
- Loyalty rewards.
- Customer accounts.
- Delivery tracking.
- Analytics dashboards.

## Critical - Never In Demo
- Real payment processing.
- Real customer data collection.
- Sending emails to real users.
- Publishing production pages from the demo machine.

## Demo Safety Rule
If a request sounds useful but is not necessary for the launch page, park it in DECISIONS.md instead of building it live.
