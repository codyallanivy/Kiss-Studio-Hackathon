# Product Vision - Northwind Brew

## One-Sentence Vision
Northwind Brew needs a calm, polished landing page that helps local coffee fans understand the brand and join the launch list.

## Audience
- Nearby customers who want a friendly, independent coffee shop.
- Remote workers looking for a comfortable neighborhood place.
- Early supporters who will join a launch list before the cafe opens.

## Brand Direction
Warm, grounded, and handmade without looking old-fashioned. The page should feel like morning light, roasted coffee, clean typography, and a small business that knows its neighborhood.

## Tier 1 Scope - Ship Now
- A one-page landing page.
- Clear hero headline and short supporting copy.
- Email signup input and primary signup button.
- One featured roast or launch card.
- Logo mark, color palette, and visual foundation assets.
- Basic accessibility checks: readable contrast, clear button label, and mobile-friendly layout.

## Tier 2 Scope - Park for Later
- Ecommerce checkout.
- Online ordering.
- Loyalty rewards.
- Events calendar.
- Customer accounts.
- Analytics dashboard.

## Out Of Scope For Now
- Real payment processing.
- Delivery tracking.
- Multi-location support.
- Inventory or staff operations.
- Anything involving live customer data.

## Acceptance Criteria
- A visitor can understand the brand in under ten seconds.
- The only action is joining the launch list.
- The visual system can be reused in Builder Studio and Creative Studio.
- Any future feature request is checked against the scope gate before work starts.
