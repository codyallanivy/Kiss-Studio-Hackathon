# Creation Brief - Northwind Brew

Northwind Brew is a neighborhood coffee brand preparing a simple launch page. The page should feel warm, clear, and trustworthy. It is for people who want to discover the cafe before it opens and join the launch list.

## Page Concept

Hero headline: Start your morning close to home.

Supporting copy: Small-batch coffee, a calm place to work, and a launch list for neighbors who want to hear when the doors open.

Primary action: Join the launch list.

## Visual Notes

- Cream background with espresso text.
- Sage and amber accents.
- Rounded cards, simple product photography feel, and clean type.
- Logo should work as a small mark in the dashboard and as a site header icon.

## Asset Needs

- Logo mark.
- Hero composition.
- Mobile signup mockup.
- Palette card.
- Simple roast card.
